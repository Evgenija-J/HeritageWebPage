Welcome to - "WHERE TO?" - your go-to destination for discovering historical landmarks and monuments! We are passionate about preserving and sharing the rich history embedded in various locations around Macedonia.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make history accessible to everyone. We believe in the power of exploration and education, and we strive to create a platform where users can seamlessly navigate through historical sites, learn about their significance, and plan their visits.

What We Offer

Explore a curated collection of landmarks, monuments, and other historical sites. Each entry provides detailed information and practical details to enhance your historical journey. Whether you're a history enthusiast, a traveler, or a student, "Where to?" is your gateway to the past.
